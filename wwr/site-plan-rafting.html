Purpose

This website is created to solve problems regarding simple mistake people make when they are planning to travel. We peovide services to tourists so they can have a fair idea about their destination,travel documents needed, hotel reservations and flight schedules. We provide extra services such visa application if applicable, ticketing to rafting site and airport pick-up. This website will help us build revenue and present tourists with the opportunity to have a feel of the locations by viewing pictures and videos, also reach out large amount of tourists who are interested in rafting.

Audience

Our target customers are tourist, students and professional atlets. Tourist are usually who visit for relaxation ans pleassure, they are usually the working class, so they can afford their vacation, in contrast, student are sponsored by guadian or instituion to visit rafting site or take part in a rafting and due to the nature of the sport, student in the university are allowed to partake in rafting. Professional athlete would visit to proctice to enhance their rafting skills. Our clients are mainly tourists who have interest in rafting, students want to learn about rafting, professional rafters and people visit to see rafters raft for pleassure Most rafting sites do not provide extra services such as accomodation, pick-up, website with detailed informatiom, document to support visa for those who require one to visit the site and online ticketing and reservation.
